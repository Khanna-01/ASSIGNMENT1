Biography

How it Started Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, is a renowned Indian cricketer. Known for his aggressive style, Kohli has achieved numerous records, including being one of the fastest to reach 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 runs in One Day Internationals (ODIs). He captained the Indian cricket team across formats and led them to significant victories. Off the field, Virat is known for his philanthropy work and fitness advocacy. He married Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma in 2017, and the couple often makes headlines as a power couple. Despite stepping down as captain in some formats, Kohli's impact on Indian cricket remains indelible.
